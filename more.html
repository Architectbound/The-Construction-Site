How We Came to Be

I, an anonymous teenager with big dreams, started coding this site because I had a vision before bed. Stupid? Yes. Cliche? Most definitely. But when I came up with this idea I knew it could have massive, diverse applications.

I could target the site, in a business setting, to all different classes of workers who contribute to the building and furnishing of modern homes. And, at the same time, I could target the site to teenagers with unlimited imaginations like me.

For a couple of weeks, I had it in my head that I could build my own house. But I needed a way to visualize it. I couldn’t find a simple, non-complex 3D sandbox — so I thought, what if I made one myself?

The only problem was that I had no clue how to code. And nobody makes it easy. So here we are — learning by building.
